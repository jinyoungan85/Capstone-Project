About our project: ClassRoomBooker

For many college students, it is often inevitable to have gaps between classes. Sometimes students are merely not able to create a desirable course schedule because of conflict between class schedules and their personal life.

Either case, having hours of a gap can be annoying and make people get frustrated more if they canâ€™t find comfortable spaces to spend the time. Our project is going to address this issue by providing real-time information on the availability of classrooms for students and further allowing them to book the available room as needed for various needs.

Share

Found empty classrooms? How about letting your friends and classmates know? Share the status of classrooms with others. It's pretty simple and easy to let the people know in few clicks or touches.

Book

Do you need a classroom for future use? Need a space for a team project? Don't bother with writing emails for booking requests anymore. Register today and try our booking service. It's very straightforward.
